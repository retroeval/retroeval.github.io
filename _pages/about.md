# About RetroEval 2026

## Venue & Travel Information
### Venue
RetroEval 2026 will be held at the [Sir Duncan Rice Library](https://www.abdn.ac.uk/library/libraries-spaces/the-sir-duncan-rice-library/) at the [University of Aberdeen](https://www.abdn.ac.uk/). The venue address is:

>*Sir Duncan Rice Library*, *Bedford Road, University of Aberdeen, Aberdeen AB24 3AA*

### Public Transport
Aberdeen is reachable by train at Aberdeen Train station and by plane at Aberdeen International Airport. The [Sir Duncan Rice Library](https://www.abdn.ac.uk/library/libraries-spaces/the-sir-duncan-rice-library/) is directly reachable via bus line *19* from the city centre.  

### Car Parking
All car parking within the University of Aberdeen is reserved for university staff and is strictly controlled. Public street parking may be available in surrounding streets around the university. 

## Contact
For private inquiries, please email us at: [retroeval@googlegroups.com](mailto:retroeval@googlegroups.com).
